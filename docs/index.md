# Ultimate Upsell & Cross Sell

**Boost your Shopify store revenue with smart upsell and cross-sell offers.**

Ultimate Upsell & Cross Sell lets you create targeted product offers that appear on your product pages and cart — powered by manual selection or AI-driven recommendations.

## Key Features

- **3 offer types** — Bundle, Popup, Cart Upsell
- **AI recommendations** — Similar products or bestsellers, automatically surfaced
- **Flexible discounts** — Percentage or fixed-amount, applied per-item or per-order
- **Full analytics** — Funnel tracking: impressions → clicks → conversions → revenue
- **Revenue-based billing** — Pay based on the revenue you generate, not a flat fee

---

## Important Notes

> **Revenue limits apply per billing cycle (30 days).** When your cycle's generated revenue reaches your plan limit, upsell offers are automatically hidden from your storefront until the cycle resets. Upgrade your plan to increase the limit.

> **Theme blocks must be manually added** to your theme via the Shopify Theme Editor before offers appear on your storefront. See [Theme Blocks](theme-blocks) for instructions.
