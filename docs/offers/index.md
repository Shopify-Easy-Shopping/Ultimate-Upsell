# Offers

Offers are the product recommendations shown to your customers on product pages and in the cart. This section covers how to create, configure, and manage offers.

## In This Section

- [Creating Offers](create) — Full walkthrough of the offer creation form
- [Offer Types](types) — Bundle, Popup, and Cart Upsell explained
- [Discounts](discounts) — Adding a special discount to your offers

## Offer Lifecycle

```
Draft → Active → Paused → Active
```

| Status | Visible to Customers | Description |
|--------|---------------------|-------------|
| **Draft** | No | Newly created offers start here. Safe to configure without affecting the storefront. |
| **Active** | Yes | Offer is live and will display to customers who meet the trigger conditions. |
| **Paused** | No | Temporarily disabled. Can be re-activated at any time without losing settings. |
